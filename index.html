Welcome to the Seforim Giveaway!

Seforim are an integral part of learning. However, they can be extremely costly and lack of access to seforim can impact a students ability to learn. For this reason, a generous donor has pledged to provide one sefer to any student in need.

Giveaway Rules

To enter the giveaway you must be:

  • A current student in yeshiva or seminary
  • Between the ages of 17-25
  • Qualify for financial aid

Note: Please use disrection when applying for this giveaway. There are limited resources that are intended for those truly in need.

Our Selection

Mesilas Yesharim

The Mesilas Yesharim is a classic work written by the Ramchal. A classic mussar sefer that is a nessesity for any learning Jew.

Iggeres Haramban

The Iggeres Haramban is a collection of letters written by the illustrious Ramban to people in ancient times. Despite its age, this sefer contains timeless wisdom for the contemporary generation.

The Stone Chumash

The Chumash is the epicenter of all Torah learning and is of critical imporantance for the bookshelf of every serious Jew.
